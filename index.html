Brave the Mystery Dungeons!

Wake up in a world of Pokémon only to discover that you’ve become a Pokémon yourself. Solve the mystery of your strange predicament by befriending other Pokémon and traveling through constantly changing dungeons. There are enemy Pokémon to battle, miraculous treasures to discover, and secrets to uncover as you continue your quest.

Which Pokémon Are You?

Depending on the game, you may take a quiz to determine the Pokemon you will be on your adventure or you may select the Pokemon you want. Find out what awaits you in these dangerous dungeons as you slowly adapt to your new self and abilities.

Choose Your Partner!

You will decide the Pokémon that will be your partner on your adventure. Together you will form a team, and form an unbreakable bond as you both help each other hand-in-hand and share in each others' joy and pain.
